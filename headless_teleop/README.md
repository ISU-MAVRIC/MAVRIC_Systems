# Controls

## Driving Controls

### Forward Controls
- `w` drive forward, normal speed
- `w + shift` drive forward, fast speed
- `w + ctrl` drive forward, slow

- `s` move backwards
- `a` steer left
- `d` steer right

### Rotation
- `q` Rotate left
- `e` Rotate right


## Arm Controls

### Claw
- `[` open claw
- `]` close claw

### Wrist
- `i` pitch up
- `k` pitch down
- `c` rotate left
- `v` rotate right

### Shoulder
- `z` rotate left
- `x` rotate right
- `y` pitch up
- `h` pitch down

### Elbow
- `u` pitch up
- `j` pitch down
